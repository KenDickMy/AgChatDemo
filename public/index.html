Tips for a Wow Demo

The difference between a forgettable demo and a wow demo is usually not the features you show — it's the business story you tell.

🎭

Lead with the Scenario

Start with the business pressure ("supply crisis hits 2 weeks before planting"), not with the product.

🚀

Move Quickly

Each step should take 2–3 minutes. Keep energy up. Narrate outcomes — not clicks.

🔁

Show Iteration Live

Demonstrate that natural language refinement ("shorter," "warmer tone") works. This builds immediate confidence.

💼

Connect to Business Outcomes

After every step, name the business impact: "This is 2 hours of analysis in 90 seconds. That's every Monday morning."
